YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR

When it comes to your personal data, you have several rights, such as
- the rights to access your data
- the rights to withdraw your consent
- the rights to rectify your data and
- the rights to object your data to be processed; and
- the rights to erasure.

If you wish to know what personal data of yours we have processed you view for free by go to "report a case" page.

If it should turn out that your details are incorrect, incomplete or irrelevant, you can submit an additional request for your details to be rectified or supplemented.

If you believe personal data of yours we have processed should be erased or withdraw your consent, you can submit to our Data Protection Officer.

You must submit this request to the organisation within the Ministry that processed the data in question.

If you have general questions about data protection at the Ministry of Justice and Security, please contact our Data Protection Officer at either:
